Financial Protection & Piece of Mind

Voyager Travels is a member of IATA, the international body. To become a member of IATA, agents must be financially viable, reputable and operate in line with IATA regulations.

IATA is a regulatory body, without IATA we would not be able to operate, many travel agents have to book through IATA accredited agents because they are unable to become members themselves. our IATA membership is an assurance to our customers that they are dealing with a well established, reputable and financially sound company which will be accountable to them at all times. Voyager Travels has its own code of ethics and is responsible to its customers at large.
